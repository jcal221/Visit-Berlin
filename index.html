Fun facts about Berlin

Street Art Scene

Berlin is renowned for its vibrant street art scene, with colorful graffiti and murals adorning many neighborhoods like Kreuzberg and Friedrichshain.

Thriving Nightlife

Berlin's nightlife is legendary, boasting a diverse club scene with world-famous techno venues like Berghain, Tresor, and Watergate.

Bridge Capital

Berlin has more bridges than Venice, with over 1,700 spanning its waterways, including the River Spree and canals, offering picturesque views.
